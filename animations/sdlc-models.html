📘 SDLC Models — Waterfall vs Agile

Same software, two philosophies. Watch how Waterfall marches once through every stage before delivering anything, while Agile delivers a small working slice every sprint — and repeats.

🌊 Waterfall

Sequential • deliver once at the very end • change is expensive
First usable software appears only after the last stage. If requirements were wrong, you find out far too late.

🔁 Agile Sprint 1

Iterative sprints • deliver working software every cycle • embraces change
Each short sprint ships a small working feature, gets feedback, and improves — risk stays tiny and constant.
